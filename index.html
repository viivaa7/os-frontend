About This Website

This platform helps monitor and analyze oil spills through AI and simulations. Here’s how it works:

  1. Upload: Users upload drone-captured images of the suspected oil spill.
  2. AI Analysis: The system processes the image, detects the spill, and estimates its area, severity, and shape.
  3. Tracking Results: Key data such as affected coordinates and estimated dispersion are displayed.
  4. Simulation: A predictive model simulates how the spill will spread over time.
  5. History: Previous uploads are stored for analysis and tracking of spill trends.
